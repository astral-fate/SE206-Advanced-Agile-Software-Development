Course Objectives

  • Understand the difference between Agile and Traditional development methods.
  • Learn Agile principles and characteristics.
  • Gain in-depth knowledge of Agile methodologies like Extreme Programming (XP) and Scrum.
  • Understand Agile roles, practices, and artifacts (User Stories, Backlogs, Sprints, etc.).
  • Explore techniques for Agile effort estimation (Story Points, COCOMO adaptations).
  • Learn about applying and evaluating Agile practices (GQM, 4-DAT).
  • Understand Requirement Engineering concepts within an Agile context.
  • Master the fundamentals of software testing within Agile environments, including verification, validation, and various testing levels and methods.
