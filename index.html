Course Objectives

  • Understand the differences between Agile and Traditional software development models (like Waterfall).
  • Explain the core values and principles outlined in the Agile Manifesto.
  • Describe the key characteristics and advantages of using Agile methodologies.
  • Identify and differentiate various Agile methodologies (XP, Scrum, FDD, DSDM, etc.).
  • Detail the lifecycle, values, roles, practices, and artifacts of Extreme Programming (XP).
  • Detail the process flow, roles, events, and artifacts of the Scrum framework.
  • Recognize the purpose of key Agile documents and artifacts (User Stories, Backlogs, Burndown Charts).
  • Understand the importance of concepts like iteration, incremental delivery, customer collaboration, and responding to change in Agile development.
